You're probably wondering...

What if Sam gives a parent wrong information?

Sam only answers based on the documents and information you provide. It doesn't make things up. If a question is outside what it knows, it tells the parent to contact your admissions office directly. You approve everything before it goes live.

Do I need to be technical to use this?

Not at all. We handle the entire setup. You just send us your handbook, tuition sheet, and any other info families usually ask about. We do the rest, including adding the chat widget to your website.

Can Sam help with admissions conversion?

Yes. Sam is designed to gently guide conversations toward scheduling a campus tour or contacting admissions — the two highest-converting actions. It also captures parent names and emails for your admissions team to follow up with.

Can I update the information Sam knows?

Absolutely. Whenever tuition changes, you add a program, or policies update — just let us know and we'll update Sam within 24 hours. It's included in your plan.
